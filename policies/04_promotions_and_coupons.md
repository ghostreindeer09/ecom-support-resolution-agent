# Promotions and Coupon Terms

**Document ID:** POL-PROMO-004
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All promotional offers, coupon codes, and discount programs

---

## Section 1: General Coupon Terms

Promotional coupon codes are issued by the platform for use on eligible purchases. Each coupon code is subject to the following general terms unless otherwise specified in the promotional communication:

1. **One coupon per order**: Only one promotional coupon code may be applied per order. Coupon codes cannot be combined with other coupon codes or stacked with other promotional offers unless the promotional terms explicitly state otherwise.
2. **Minimum purchase requirement**: Some coupons require a minimum purchase amount before the discount is applied. The minimum applies to the subtotal of eligible items before taxes and shipping.
3. **Expiration**: All coupon codes have an expiration date, which is displayed in the promotional communication and at checkout. Expired codes cannot be honored under any circumstances.
4. **Single use**: Unless specified as multi-use, each coupon code can be used once per customer account.
5. **Non-transferable**: Coupon codes are issued to specific customer accounts or email addresses and cannot be transferred to other accounts.
6. **No cash value**: Coupon codes have no cash value and cannot be redeemed for cash or store credit.

---

## Section 2: Retroactive Coupon Application

**Coupon codes cannot be applied retroactively to orders that have already been placed.** If a customer forgot to apply a coupon code at checkout or the code did not apply correctly, the following policy applies:

1. The order cannot be modified after submission to add a coupon code
2. Customer support agents are **not authorized** to manually apply discounts or issue partial refunds to match a coupon value
3. If the coupon code is still valid and has not expired, the customer may cancel the order (if it has not shipped) and place a new order with the coupon applied
4. If the order has already shipped, the customer must complete the order and may use the coupon on a future order if it has not expired

**Exception**: If the platform's checkout system experienced a documented technical error that prevented a valid coupon from being applied, the platform will issue a refund for the difference. This requires verification by the technical support team and the customer must provide evidence (screenshot, error message) of the failed coupon application during checkout.

---

## Section 3: Promotional Sale Events

During platform-wide promotional events (seasonal sales, flash sales, clearance events):

1. All sale prices are final at the time of purchase
2. Price adjustments will not be provided if an item goes on sale after the customer's purchase or if the sale price decreases further during the event
3. Items purchased during promotional events are subject to the same return policy as regular-price items, unless the item is marked as "Final Sale"
4. "Final Sale" items purchased during promotional events are **non-returnable and non-refundable**
5. Promotional pricing errors may be corrected, and orders placed at incorrect prices may be cancelled with a full refund — see the Cancellation Policy, Section 5

---

## Section 4: VIP and Loyalty Program Offers

VIP offers and loyalty program discounts are issued to eligible customers based on their account history and program tier. These offers:

1. Are tied to the customer's account and cannot be transferred
2. Have specific redemption requirements that are communicated in the offer email or notification
3. May require a minimum loyalty points balance to activate
4. Are verified against the customer's account at checkout — if the offer cannot be verified, it will not be applied
5. Customers who believe they received a VIP offer that is not being recognized at checkout should provide a screenshot of the offer email or notification for verification by customer support
6. Unverifiable promotional claims (where the customer claims to have received an offer but cannot provide documentation) cannot be honored

---

## Section 5: Gift with Purchase

Some promotions include a free gift with a qualifying purchase:

1. The gift item is included at the discretion of the platform and is subject to availability
2. If the qualifying purchase is returned, the gift item must also be returned
3. If the gift item is not returned with the qualifying purchase, its retail value will be deducted from the refund amount
4. Gift items are subject to the same return policy as regular items if returned independently (without returning the qualifying purchase)

---

## Section 6: Referral Credits

Customer referral credits are issued in the form of store credit when a referred customer makes their first qualifying purchase:

1. Referral credits are typically $10–$25 depending on the active referral program terms
2. Credits are applied to the referring customer's account within 14 days of the referred customer's qualifying purchase
3. Referral credits expire 90 days after issuance
4. Referral credits cannot be exchanged for cash
5. If the referred customer's qualifying purchase is returned, the referral credit will be revoked from the referring customer's account
