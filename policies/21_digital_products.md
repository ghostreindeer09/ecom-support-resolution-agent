# Digital Products and Software Policy

**Document ID:** POL-DIG-021
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All digital downloads, software licenses, and digital subscriptions

---

## Section 1: Digital Product Categories

Digital products sold on the platform include:

1. **Software licenses**: Desktop applications, productivity software, antivirus programs, design tools
2. **Digital downloads**: E-books, music albums, movie downloads, audiobooks
3. **Game licenses**: PC and console game download codes
4. **Digital subscriptions**: Software-as-a-Service (SaaS) subscriptions, streaming subscriptions
5. **Digital gift cards**: Email-delivered gift card codes
6. **Online courses**: Educational content and training programs

Each category may have specific return and refund terms as outlined in this policy.

---

## Section 2: Return and Refund Policy for Digital Products

Digital products are generally **non-returnable** once the product key has been activated, the download has been initiated, or the seal has been broken. This is because digital products can be fully consumed (copied, installed, or used) before a return is initiated, unlike physical goods.

### 2.1 Software with Sealed Packaging
Physical software packages (boxed software with a product key inside) follow these rules:
- **Unopened, seal intact**: Full refund within the standard 30-day return window
- **Opened, seal broken, key not activated**: Refund may be considered at discretion — the customer must demonstrate the key has not been activated (verification with the software publisher may be required)
- **Opened, key activated**: **Non-returnable**. The customer has consumed the product by activating the license. Direct the customer to the software publisher's support for technical issues.

### 2.2 Digital Download Products
- **Before download**: Full refund if the customer requests cancellation before initiating the download
- **After download**: **Non-refundable**. The customer has received the digital content.
- **Exception**: If the downloaded file is corrupted, does not work as described, or the customer receives a duplicate purchase notification, a refund may be issued after verification

### 2.3 Game License Codes
- **Unredeemed codes**: Full refund within 14 days of purchase
- **Redeemed codes**: **Non-refundable**. Once a game code is redeemed on a gaming platform (Steam, PlayStation Network, Xbox Live, etc.), the code cannot be un-redeemed and the purchase is final.

### 2.4 Digital Subscriptions
- See Returns and Refunds Policy, Section 8 (Subscription Services) for subscription cancellation and refund terms
- Digital subscriptions may offer a free trial period. If the customer cancels during the free trial, no charges apply. Charges that occur after the trial period has ended are subject to standard subscription refund policy.

## Section 3: Defective Digital Products

If a digital product is defective (software crashes, file is corrupted, product does not function as described):

1. **First step**: Direct the customer to the software publisher or content creator's support channel for technical assistance
2. **If publisher support is unavailable or unhelpful**: The platform may issue a store credit equivalent to the purchase price
3. **For verified platform-side errors** (e.g., wrong product delivered, wrong version, incorrect file format): Full refund will be issued regardless of download or activation status
4. **For compatibility issues** (product does not work with the customer's hardware or operating system): Not eligible for refund unless the product listing failed to specify compatibility requirements that were not met

---

## Section 4: Pre-Purchase Information Requirements

All digital product listings must clearly display:

1. System requirements (for software)
2. File format and size (for downloads)
3. DRM (Digital Rights Management) restrictions
4. Whether the product requires an internet connection
5. License type (single-user, multi-device, family plan)
6. Refund eligibility status (returnable vs. non-returnable)
7. Subscription terms (if applicable — billing frequency, cancellation process, trial period)

If any of these disclosures are missing from the product listing and the customer's claim is related to the undisclosed information, the customer may be eligible for a refund regardless of the standard digital product return restrictions. Cases involving inadequate product disclosures should be evaluated carefully and may require escalation.

---

## Section 5: Software License Transfer

Customers may inquire about transferring their software license to another person:

1. License transferability depends on the software publisher's terms, not the platform's policy
2. The platform cannot facilitate license transfers between customers
3. The platform cannot guarantee that transferred licenses will continue to work
4. If a customer wants to "give away" software they purchased, they should contact the software publisher about license transfer options

---

## Section 6: Region-Locked Digital Products

Some digital products are region-locked and can only be activated or used in specific geographic regions:

1. Region restrictions are displayed on the product listing page
2. If a customer purchases a region-locked product while in an eligible region but later moves to a non-eligible region, the platform cannot issue a refund for the region incompatibility
3. If a region restriction was NOT disclosed on the product listing and the customer is unable to use the product, a full refund will be issued
4. VPN usage to circumvent region restrictions voids any warranty or return eligibility for the product

---

## Section 7: Digital Content Disputes

When a customer disputes a digital product purchase:

1. **Unauthorized purchase**: If the customer claims they did not make the purchase, follow the fraud investigation process
2. **Duplicate purchase**: If the customer accidentally purchased the same digital product twice, one purchase may be refunded if the second copy/code has not been used
3. **Content not as described**: If the digital content does not match the listing description, the customer may be eligible for a refund with evidence of the discrepancy
4. **Quality dispute**: Subjective quality complaints ("I didn't like the movie," "The book was boring") are not eligible for refund
