# Missing, Incomplete, and Incorrect Orders Policy

**Document ID:** POL-MISS-024
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All orders where items are missing, incomplete, or incorrect upon delivery

---

## Section 1: Missing Items from a Multi-Item Order

When a customer receives an order but one or more items are missing from the shipment:

### 1.1 Separate Shipments
Multi-item orders may be shipped in separate packages from different fulfillment centers. Before processing a missing item claim:
1. Check if the order was split into multiple shipments — each shipment will have its own tracking number
2. Verify the tracking status of all shipments in the order
3. If a separate shipment is still in transit, inform the customer of the expected delivery date for the remaining items
4. If a separate shipment shows as delivered but the customer did not receive it, process as a lost package claim

### 1.2 Missing Item in a Single Shipment
If the order was shipped in a single package and one or more items are missing:
1. Ask the customer to verify all items in the package, including checking under padding materials and inside smaller compartments
2. Ask for a photo of the packing slip (if included) showing all items listed
3. If the packing slip shows the item was included but the customer confirms it is not in the package, process a **fulfillment error claim**
4. If the packing slip does not list the item, the item may not have been included due to an inventory issue — process as an **out-of-stock partial fulfillment**

### 1.3 Resolution for Missing Items
- **Fulfillment error**: Reship the missing item at no cost with expedited shipping. If the item is out of stock, issue a full refund for the missing item.
- **Out-of-stock partial fulfillment**: Issue a full refund for the missing item. If the customer was charged shipping based on the full order, recalculate and refund any excess shipping charges.
- **All items missing (empty package)**: This is a rare event that suggests a fulfillment or carrier issue. Reship the entire order with expedited shipping and escalate to the fulfillment operations team for investigation. If the customer prefers a refund, process a full refund.

## Section 2: Incorrect Items Received

When a customer receives an item that is different from what they ordered:

### 2.1 Wrong Product Entirely
The customer received a completely different product (e.g., ordered a camera, received a kitchen mixer):
1. Process as a fulfillment error — this is always a platform mistake
2. Provide a prepaid return label for the incorrect item
3. Ship the correct item immediately (do not wait for the incorrect item to be returned)
4. If the correct item is out of stock, issue a full refund plus a 10% goodwill credit

### 2.2 Wrong Variant (Size, Color, Model)
The customer received the right product type but the wrong variant:
1. Verify the discrepancy against the order details
2. If confirmed, provide a prepaid return label and ship the correct variant
3. If the correct variant is out of stock, offer alternative options or a full refund

### 2.3 Wrong Quantity
The customer received more or fewer units than ordered:
- **Fewer than ordered**: Reship the missing units or offer a partial refund
- **More than ordered**: The customer may keep the extra items or return them; the customer will not be charged for extra items received in error

## Section 3: Partial Delivery Issues

Sometimes an order arrives in an incomplete state — not due to missing items, but due to incomplete product condition:

### 3.1 Missing Accessories or Components
If the product is missing included accessories listed on the product page (e.g., charger cable, remote control, mounting hardware):
1. Check if the accessories were supposed to be included per the product listing
2. If yes, arrange for the missing accessories to be shipped separately
3. If the accessories cannot be sourced separately, offer a partial refund (10–20% of the purchase price) or a full return and refund
4. Missing accessories that are critical to the product's function (e.g., power adapter) should be shipped with expedited delivery

### 3.2 Incomplete Assembly Parts
For items requiring assembly that arrive with missing hardware or parts:
1. Contact the manufacturer or fulfillment partner for replacement parts
2. If replacement parts can be shipped within 5 business days, offer this option to the customer
3. If parts will take longer than 5 business days, offer a full return and refund
4. If the customer has partially assembled the item, they may still return it — the platform will arrange pickup of the partially assembled item

## Section 4: Order Accuracy Disputes

When the customer and the order record disagree about what was ordered:

### 4.1 Customer Claims Different Item Was Ordered
If the customer says they ordered item A but the order record shows item B:
1. Review the order history and the product page at the time of purchase
2. If the product page or order process was misleading (e.g., variant selector was confusing), offer a goodwill exchange or refund
3. If the order record is clear and the customer selected the item they received, kindly explain the order details
4. If there is a genuine ambiguity, escalate to a supervisor

### 4.2 Customer Claims Order Was Modified Without Consent
If the customer believes their order was changed after placement:
1. Review the order modification log
2. If a substitution was made (items out of stock, replaced with a similar item), verify that the customer was notified and consented to the substitution
3. If the customer did not consent to a substitution, process a full refund for the substituted item
4. Unauthorized modifications should be escalated for investigation

## Section 5: Reporting Windows

| Issue Type | Reporting Window |
|---|---|
| Missing item from shipment | 14 calendar days from delivery |
| Wrong item received | 14 calendar days from delivery |
| Missing accessories/components | 30 calendar days from delivery |
| Extra items received | No deadline — customer may notify at any time |
| Partial fulfillment (out of stock) | Automatic notification at time of shipment |

Claims filed outside these windows will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis. Extenuating circumstances may be considered.

---

## Section 6: Repeated Order Accuracy Issues

If a customer experiences multiple order accuracy issues (2+ within a 6-month period):

1. Escalate the pattern to the fulfillment operations team
2. Investigate whether the issues share a common cause (same fulfillment center, same carrier, same product category)
3. Offer the customer a goodwill gesture (store credit) in addition to resolving the immediate issue
4. Document the pattern for quality improvement initiatives
5. If the customer has experienced 3+ order accuracy issues, assign a dedicated agent for their next interaction to ensure personalized service
