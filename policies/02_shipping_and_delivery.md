# Shipping and Delivery Policy

**Document ID:** POL-SD-002
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All orders placed through the platform (first-party and marketplace)

---

## Section 1: Standard Shipping Timelines

All first-party orders are processed and shipped within **1–2 business days** from the date of order placement. Business days are Monday through Friday, excluding federal holidays. Orders placed after 2:00 PM local time may be processed on the next business day.

Estimated delivery windows vary by shipping method:
- **Standard Shipping:** 5–7 business days from ship date
- **Expedited Shipping:** 2–3 business days from ship date
- **Next-Day Shipping:** 1 business day from ship date (orders placed before 12:00 PM)
- **Economy Shipping:** 7–14 business days from ship date

Delivery estimates are provided at checkout and in the order confirmation email. These estimates are not guarantees and may be affected by carrier delays, weather events, customs processing (for international orders), and other factors beyond our control.

Marketplace seller shipping timelines may vary. Each marketplace seller sets their own processing and shipping times, which are displayed on the product listing page. The platform does not guarantee marketplace seller shipping timelines but monitors seller performance to ensure reasonable delivery times.

## Section 2: Lost Package Policy

A package is considered **potentially lost** when it meets one of the following criteria:

1. Tracking has shown no movement for **7 consecutive calendar days** while still in transit
2. The estimated delivery date has passed by **10 or more calendar days** with no delivery confirmation
3. Tracking information shows the package was returned to the shipper without delivery attempt

When a package is deemed lost:
- For **first-party orders**: The customer is entitled to a full refund or free reshipment of the order at their choice. The refund or reshipment will be processed within 2–3 business days of the lost package determination. No return of the original item is required (since it was never received).
- For **marketplace orders**: The customer should first contact the marketplace seller. If the seller does not respond within 48 hours or refuses to resolve the issue, the platform's **Marketplace Guarantee** will cover a full refund.

Lost package claims must be filed within **60 calendar days** of the original order date. Claims filed after 60 days may not be eligible for resolution.

## Section 3: Carrier Confirmation of Delivery

When a carrier (USPS, UPS, FedEx, DHL, or other contracted carrier) confirms delivery of a package — including status updates such as "Delivered," "Left at Front Door," "Delivered to Mailbox," or "Signed For" — the platform considers the order as fulfilled.

If a customer claims non-receipt despite carrier confirmation of delivery:
1. The customer should first check with household members and neighbors
2. Check the delivery location specified in tracking (e.g., front door, mailbox, reception)
3. Wait **48 hours** — some carriers mark packages as delivered prematurely
4. If the package is still not located after 48 hours, the customer may file a **delivery dispute**

**Delivery disputes** where the carrier shows confirmed delivery (especially with signature confirmation) are treated as high-priority investigations. These cases require:
- A signed declaration from the customer stating the package was not received
- Review of carrier's GPS delivery data (if available)
- Cross-reference with the delivery address on file

If the carrier provides **signature confirmation** and the signature does not match the customer's name on the account, the case will be investigated as potential porch theft or delivery error. If the signature matches or the delivery is GPS-verified, the claim may be denied.

**Important:** Customers who repeatedly file delivery disputes (more than 2 within a 6-month period for the same delivery address) will be flagged for fraud review. Repeated false delivery dispute claims may result in account restrictions.

## Section 4: Address Change After Order Placement

Customers may request a change of delivery address under the following conditions:

1. **Before shipment** (order_status = "placed" or "processing"): The address can be changed through the self-service portal or by contacting support. No additional charges apply.
2. **After shipment** (order_status = "shipped"): Address changes are **not possible** once the package is in transit with the carrier. The platform cannot redirect packages that are already in the carrier's possession. Customers should contact the carrier directly for package intercept services, which may incur additional fees charged by the carrier.
3. **International orders**: Address changes for international orders must be made within 2 hours of order placement due to customs documentation requirements.

If a package is delivered to the wrong address because the customer provided an incorrect address at checkout, the platform is not responsible for the loss. The customer may file a claim with the carrier.

---

## Section 5: Shipping Damage

If a package arrives with visible damage to the outer packaging or the item inside:

1. The customer should photograph the damaged packaging and item before discarding anything
2. File a damage claim within **7 calendar days** of delivery — see Returns and Refunds Policy, Section 3
3. The customer may be asked to retain the packaging for carrier inspection

For items with a purchase value above $200, the carrier may arrange an in-person inspection before the claim is processed. This inspection will be scheduled at the customer's convenience and at no cost to the customer.

---

## Section 6: International Shipping

International orders are subject to the following additional policies:

1. Customs duties, taxes, and import fees are the **responsibility of the customer** and are not included in the item price or shipping charges
2. Delivery timelines for international orders are estimates only and can be significantly impacted by customs processing
3. If an international package is held by customs, the platform will provide documentation to help the customer clear the package but cannot pay customs fees on the customer's behalf
4. International returns follow the same return window but the customer is responsible for return shipping costs unless the return is due to a platform error
5. Some products may be restricted from international shipping due to export regulations

---

## Section 7: Marketplace Seller Shipping

Marketplace sellers are independent third parties that sell through the platform. They are responsible for:

1. Setting their own shipping rates and delivery timelines
2. Fulfilling and shipping orders placed through their storefront
3. Handling shipping-related customer inquiries as the first point of contact

The platform provides a **Marketplace Guarantee** that protects customers when marketplace sellers fail to meet their obligations:

1. If a marketplace seller does not ship an order within their stated processing time (plus a 3-day grace period), the customer may cancel the order for a full refund through the platform
2. If a marketplace order is not delivered by the estimated delivery date plus 10 days, the customer may file a claim under the Marketplace Guarantee
3. The Marketplace Guarantee covers up to the full purchase price plus shipping charges
4. The Marketplace Guarantee does not apply if the customer provided an incorrect address or if the package was confirmed delivered by the carrier

For marketplace orders with shipping disputes, the platform will mediate between the customer and seller. If the seller refuses to cooperate, the platform will issue a refund under the Marketplace Guarantee and pursue reimbursement from the seller separately.
