# Payment and Billing Policy

**Document ID:** POL-PAY-005
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All payment transactions on the platform

---

## Section 1: Accepted Payment Methods

The platform accepts the following payment methods:
1. Credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover)
2. Debit cards with Visa or Mastercard network
3. Platform store credit and gift cards
4. PayPal
5. Apple Pay and Google Pay
6. Buy Now, Pay Later services (Affirm, Klarna, Afterpay) — where available
7. Wire transfer (for orders above $5,000, by arrangement)

All payment information is processed securely using industry-standard encryption. The platform does not store full credit card numbers on its servers. Tokenized payment information is retained for repeat purchases with the customer's consent.

---

## Section 2: Payment Authorization and Capture

When a customer places an order, the payment method is **authorized** for the order total. Authorization places a hold on the funds but does not complete the charge. The actual **charge (capture)** occurs when the order is shipped.

If the authorization expires before shipping (typically after 7 days), the payment method will be re-authorized. If re-authorization fails, the customer will be notified and given 48 hours to update their payment method. If no update is received, the order will be cancelled.

For multi-item orders that ship in multiple packages, each shipment is charged separately as it ships. The customer may see multiple charges that add up to the original order total.

---

## Section 3: Double Charges and Billing Errors

If a customer is charged twice for the same order, the following process applies:

1. The customer should check their payment statement to confirm two separate charges (not a pending authorization and a completed charge, which is normal)
2. If a true double charge is confirmed, the duplicate charge will be refunded within **3–5 business days**
3. Platform support agents can verify double charges by checking the payment transaction log
4. If the customer disputes the charge with their bank (chargeback) before contacting the platform, the platform will cooperate with the bank investigation but the resolution timeline will follow the bank's process, which may take 30–90 days

**Important**: Pending authorizations that appear as charges on the customer's statement are **not double charges**. These authorizations typically drop off within 3–5 business days if the order is cancelled or modified.

---

## Section 4: Chargebacks and Payment Disputes

If a customer files a chargeback with their payment provider:

1. The platform will be notified by the payment processor and will review the transaction
2. The platform will respond to the chargeback with relevant evidence (order confirmation, delivery tracking, customer communication)
3. During the chargeback investigation, the order will be placed on hold
4. If the chargeback is found in the customer's favor, the refund is processed through the bank
5. If the chargeback is found in the platform's favor, the charge remains

**Policy on pre-chargeback resolution**: Customers are encouraged to contact platform support before filing a chargeback. Many disputes can be resolved faster through direct communication. If a resolution is offered and the customer still files a chargeback, the platform reserves the right to charge back any refund or credit already issued.

Customers who file **3 or more chargebacks** within a 12-month period may have their account flagged for review and may be required to use alternative payment methods for future orders.

---

## Section 5: Refund Timeline by Payment Method

Refund processing times vary by payment method:

| Payment Method | Refund Timeline |
|---|---|
| Credit card | 5–10 business days |
| Debit card | 5–10 business days |
| PayPal | 3–5 business days |
| Store credit | Instant |
| Gift card | 3–5 business days (credited back to gift card) |
| Buy Now, Pay Later | Varies by provider (typically 5–14 business days) |

The refund initiation occurs within 1–2 business days of the refund being approved. The above timelines represent the total time from approval to the funds appearing in the customer's account.

---

## Section 6: Undisclosed Fees

The platform is committed to transparent pricing. All fees, including restocking fees, return shipping costs, and any other charges, must be disclosed to the customer at the point of sale or in the order confirmation:

1. If a fee was not disclosed at checkout and the customer disputes it, the fee should be waived and the case should be escalated to management for policy review
2. Restocking fees must be listed in the product description or terms of sale. If a restocking fee was not disclosed and is disputed, it should be reversed
3. Hidden fees or undisclosed charges violate the platform's terms of service and may also violate consumer protection laws. Cases involving undisclosed fees should be treated with urgency and escalated to the compliance team
