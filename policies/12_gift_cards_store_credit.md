# Gift Card and Store Credit Policy

**Document ID:** POL-GC-012
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All gift card purchases and store credit issuances

---

## Section 1: Gift Card Terms

Gift cards purchased through the platform are subject to the following terms:

1. **Non-refundable**: Gift cards cannot be returned or refunded for cash or credit under any circumstances
2. **Non-transferable for cash**: Gift cards cannot be exchanged for cash, check, or bank transfer. This applies regardless of the remaining balance and regardless of state-specific cash-out laws (which, if cited by the customer, should be escalated to legal compliance)
3. **No expiration**: Gift cards do not expire. The balance remains available indefinitely
4. **Balance inquiries**: Customers may check their gift card balance through the platform website or by contacting customer support
5. **Lost or stolen gift cards**: The platform is not responsible for lost or stolen gift cards. Gift cards are treated as cash equivalents — if the card or code is lost, the balance cannot be recovered
6. **Denominations**: Gift cards are available in denominations from $10 to $500
7. **Usage**: Gift cards can be used for any purchase on the platform, including marketplace seller items. They cannot be used to purchase other gift cards.

---

## Section 2: Store Credit

Store credit is issued in the following circumstances:

1. **Refund to store credit**: When a refund is issued and the original payment method is unavailable
2. **Goodwill credit**: Issued as a customer satisfaction gesture (e.g., late delivery compensation)
3. **Promotional credit**: Issued as part of promotions, referral programs, or loyalty rewards
4. **Defective hygiene product credit**: When an opened hygiene product is confirmed defective — credit is issued in lieu of a refund

Store credit terms:
- Store credit expires **12 months** from the date of issuance
- Store credit cannot be exchanged for cash
- Multiple store credits can be combined in a single order
- Store credit is applied before other payment methods at checkout
- Remaining balance after an order is retained as store credit

---

## Section 3: Gift Card Disputes

If a customer disputes a gift card-related issue:

1. **Incorrect balance**: If a customer claims their gift card balance is incorrect, support can review the transaction history on the card and provide a statement
2. **Gift card not working**: If a gift card code is not being accepted at checkout, support should verify the code and check for technical issues
3. **Unauthorized use**: If a customer believes their gift card was used without authorization, the case should be treated as a potential fraud case and escalated
4. **Duplicate purchase**: If a customer accidentally purchases two gift cards, one may be refunded if it has not been redeemed

---

## Section 4: Corporate and Bulk Gift Card Orders

Corporate gift card orders (25+ cards) are handled through the business sales team and are subject to separate terms and conditions. Customer support should direct corporate gift card inquiries to business@platform.com.
