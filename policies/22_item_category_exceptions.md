# Item Category Exceptions and Special Handling

**Document ID:** POL-CATEX-022
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All items requiring category-specific handling beyond standard policy

---

## Section 1: Electronics — Extended Guidance

Electronics items (computers, phones, tablets, cameras, TVs, audio equipment, smart home devices) have the following specific policies:

### 1.1 Return Window and Conditions
- Standard 30-day return window applies
- Electronics must be returned with all original accessories, cables, manuals, and packaging
- A restocking fee of up to 15% may apply for opened electronics that are not defective
- Electronics with missing serial number stickers or altered serial numbers will not be accepted for return

### 1.2 Data Removal
- Customers are responsible for removing all personal data from returned electronics (factory reset)
- The platform is not responsible for personal data left on returned devices
- Returned devices found to contain personal data will be factory reset at the fulfillment center

### 1.3 Batteries and Hazardous Components
- Electronics containing lithium batteries must follow specific return shipping procedures
- Items with damaged or swollen batteries should NOT be shipped — the customer should contact support for safe disposal guidance
- The customer will receive a full refund for electronics with battery safety issues without requiring return

### 1.4 Manufacturer Recall
- In the event of a manufacturer recall, all recall-affected electronics will be accepted for return regardless of the return window
- The platform will notify all affected customers proactively
- See also: Warranty and Manufacturer Claims Policy

## Section 2: Apparel and Footwear

Apparel and footwear items have the following specific policies:

### 2.1 Fit and Size Returns
- Customers may return apparel for fit or size reasons within the standard 30-day window
- Items must be unworn, with all original tags attached
- Items showing signs of wear (body odor, deodorant marks, pet hair, wrinkles from wearing) will not be accepted for return and may be returned to the customer at their expense
- Footwear must be tried on indoors only — shoes showing outdoor wear marks on the sole will not be accepted

### 2.2 Sale and Clearance Apparel
- Apparel purchased during seasonal clearance events may be marked as Final Sale
- Final Sale apparel follows the Final Sale and Clearance Policy
- Non-Final-Sale clearance apparel follows the standard return policy

### 2.3 Swimwear and Intimates
- See Hygiene Products Policy — swimwear and intimates are classified as hygiene products
- Swimwear and intimates with broken hygiene seals are non-returnable
- Swimwear and intimates with intact hygiene seals may be returned within 30 days

### 2.4 Luxury and Designer Apparel
- Items priced above $500 require return authorization from the luxury fulfillment team
- Returns are inspected by specialized staff for signs of wear or alteration
- Altered items (hemmed, tailored) are non-returnable
- Originally authenticated items that are returned and fail re-authentication will be investigated and the customer may be contacted

## Section 3: Furniture and Large Items

Furniture and large items (appliances, exercise equipment, outdoor furniture) have modified return terms:

### 3.1 Return Process
- A return shipping label is not sufficient for large items — the platform will arrange a carrier pickup from the customer's location
- Pickup scheduling typically requires 3–5 business days
- The customer must ensure the item is accessible for carrier pickup (ground floor, clear path to the item)
- If the item has been assembled, the customer is not required to disassemble it, but the carrier may require it to fit in the transport vehicle

### 3.2 Restocking Fees
- Furniture and large items are subject to a restocking fee of up to 20% for non-defective returns
- The restocking fee covers the cost of return logistics, inspection, and repackaging
- If the item is defective or was damaged during delivery, no restocking fee applies

### 3.3 White Glove Delivery
- Items delivered with white glove service (assembly included) may have a higher return logistics cost
- If the customer wants to return a white-glove-delivered item, the disassembly and pickup service will be arranged at no additional charge if the return is due to a platform error or defect
- For non-defective returns of white-glove items, a disassembly fee may apply in addition to the restocking fee

### 3.4 Mattresses
- Mattresses have a unique 100-night trial period (significantly longer than the standard 30-day window)
- During the trial period, the customer may return the mattress for a full refund
- The platform will arrange pickup of the returned mattress at no charge
- Returned mattresses are donated to charity, not restocked — there is no restocking fee
- After the 100-night trial, the standard manufacturer warranty applies

## Section 4: Perishable Items — Cross-Reference

Perishable items follow the Perishable Items Policy (POL-PERISH-008). Key differences from standard returns:
- 48-hour reporting window (vs. 30 days)
- No return required (keep-item policy)
- Refund and keep applies only to perishable components
- See Perishable Items Policy for full details

## Section 5: Customized and Personalized Items

Items that have been customized or personalized to the customer's specifications:

1. **Non-returnable**: Customized items are non-returnable for change of mind or buyer's remorse
2. **Exception — Production error**: If the customization does not match the customer's submitted specifications (wrong engraving, wrong color choice, wrong monogram), the item may be returned for a full refund or remade at no charge
3. **Exception — Defective product**: If the underlying product is defective regardless of customization, the standard defect policy applies
4. **Proofing**: For items where a digital proof was provided and approved by the customer, claims of "not what I expected" are generally not eligible for return unless the physical product materially differs from the approved proof

---

## Section 6: Subscription Boxes — Cross-Reference

Subscription box orders follow the Returns and Refunds Policy, Section 8. Key differences:
- Individual items from a subscription box may have limited return eligibility depending on the subscription terms
- Full-box returns (all items from a single delivery) follow standard return procedures
- Multiple consecutive incorrect deliveries are escalated per the multi-order dispute procedures

## Section 7: Gift Cards — Cross-Reference

Gift cards follow the Gift Card and Store Credit Policy (POL-GC-012):
- Non-refundable, non-exchangeable for cash
- No expiration
- Cannot be used to purchase other gift cards
- Lost/stolen cards cannot be replaced
