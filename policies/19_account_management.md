# Account Management and Customer Rights

**Document ID:** POL-ACCT-019
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All registered customer accounts on the platform

---

## Section 1: Account Creation and Verification

Customers must create an account to make purchases on the platform. Account creation requires:

1. A valid email address (verified through a confirmation email)
2. A password meeting minimum security requirements (8+ characters, at least one uppercase letter, one number)
3. First name and last name
4. Optional: phone number, date of birth, address

Customers may create only one account per email address. Multiple accounts created by the same individual may be flagged for review and may be consolidated into a single account.

---

## Section 2: Customer Rights

All registered customers have the following rights:

1. **Right to information**: Customers may request a full record of their purchase history, account activity, and any data the platform holds about them
2. **Right to return**: Subject to the Returns and Refunds Policy, customers have the right to return eligible items within the stated return window
3. **Right to dispute**: Customers may dispute any charge, delivery, or transaction through the platform's dispute resolution process
4. **Right to escalation**: If a customer is dissatisfied with a resolution, they may request escalation to a supervisor
5. **Right to cancel**: Customers may cancel their account at any time — see Section 5
6. **Right to privacy**: Customer data is handled in accordance with the platform's Privacy Policy and applicable data protection regulations

---

## Section 3: Account Flags and Restrictions

Customer accounts may be flagged or restricted under the following circumstances:

### 3.1 Warning Flags (Informational)
- Frequent return alerts: 3+ returns within 90 days
- Address verification needed: multiple shipping addresses added in a short period
- Payment method verification: new or unusual payment method added

Warning flags do not restrict the customer's ability to place orders or make returns. They provide context for support agents handling the customer's cases.

### 3.2 Review Required
- Return abuse suspected: pattern of purchasing, using, and returning items
- Delivery dispute pattern: 2+ delivery disputes at the same address within 6 months
- Chargeback history: 2+ chargebacks within 12 months

Review Required status means that certain actions (refunds above $100, multiple returns) require supervisor approval before processing.

### 3.3 Restricted
- Confirmed fraud: account involved in verified fraudulent activity
- Excessive chargebacks: 3+ chargebacks within 12 months
- Policy abuse: confirmed pattern of return policy abuse

Restricted accounts may be limited to certain payment methods, may have delayed refund processing, or may be temporarily suspended.

## Section 4: Account Data and Privacy

The platform collects and stores the following customer data:

1. **Profile information**: Name, email, phone number, addresses
2. **Order history**: All orders placed, including items, prices, and delivery details
3. **Communication history**: Messages exchanged with support agents
4. **Payment tokens**: Tokenized payment method information (not full card numbers)
5. **Activity logs**: Login history, browsing history (with consent), search history

Customers may request:
- A copy of all data held about them (Data Subject Access Request — processed within 30 days)
- Deletion of their personal data (Right to Erasure — processed within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements)
- Correction of inaccurate data (processed within 5 business days)

---

## Section 5: Account Closure

Customers may close their account at any time by:
1. Contacting customer support
2. Using the self-service account settings page

Upon account closure:
1. All personal data will be anonymized within 30 days, subject to legal retention requirements
2. Outstanding orders will be fulfilled
3. Pending refunds will be processed
4. Store credit balance will be forfeited unless the customer requests disbursement before closure
5. Gift card balances remain active — gift cards are not linked to customer accounts
6. The customer's purchase history will be retained in anonymized form for analytics purposes

Closed accounts cannot be reactivated. The customer must create a new account to make future purchases.

## Section 6: Customer Loyalty Tiers

The platform maintains a loyalty program with the following tiers:

1. **Standard**: All customers start at the Standard tier
2. **Silver**: Customers who have spent $500+ in the last 12 months
3. **Gold**: Customers who have spent $2,000+ in the last 12 months
4. **Platinum**: Customers who have spent $5,000+ in the last 12 months

Loyalty tier benefits:
- Extended return windows (Silver: 35 days, Gold: 45 days, Platinum: 60 days)
- Priority support access (Gold and Platinum)
- Free return shipping on all returns (Gold and Platinum)
- Annual goodwill credit (Platinum: $50/year)

Loyalty tier status does not affect the customer's fundamental rights under consumer protection laws and does not override stated policy exceptions (e.g., non-returnable items remain non-returnable regardless of tier).

---

## Section 7: Vulnerable Customer Protocol

Support agents should be aware that some customers may be in vulnerable circumstances, including:

1. Elderly customers who may not be familiar with online shopping processes
2. Customers with disabilities who may have specific communication needs
3. Customers experiencing financial hardship
4. Customers in emergency situations (e.g., medication orders that didn't arrive)

For vulnerable customers:
- Provide additional patience and clarity in communications
- Offer to escalate to a supervisor who can explore flexible options
- Document any vulnerability indicators in the case notes (without judgment)
- Never exploit a customer's lack of understanding of their rights
