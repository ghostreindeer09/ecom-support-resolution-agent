# Product Listing Accuracy and Seller Obligations

**Document ID:** POL-LISTING-027
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All product listings on the platform, both first-party and marketplace

---

## Section 1: First-Party Listing Standards

All first-party product listings must include accurate and complete information:

### 1.1 Required Product Information
1. **Product title**: Clear, descriptive title that accurately identifies the product
2. **Product description**: Detailed description of the product's features, materials, dimensions, and intended use
3. **Product images**: At least 3 high-quality photographs showing the product from different angles. Images must represent the actual product, not a different variant or prototype.
4. **Price**: Clear display of the purchase price, including any applicable base fees. Taxes and shipping are calculated at checkout.
5. **Availability**: Current stock status (in stock, limited stock, out of stock, pre-order)
6. **Return eligibility**: Clear indication if the item is non-returnable or Final Sale
7. **Category-specific requirements**: Electronics must list specifications and system requirements; apparel must list materials, sizes, and care instructions; food items must list ingredients and allergen information

### 1.2 Image Accuracy Requirements
Product images must:
- Show the actual product being sold (not a similar model or different generation)
- Accurately represent colors with reasonable allowance for monitor/screen variation
- Not include accessories or items not included in the purchase unless clearly labeled as "sold separately"
- Not use lifestyle photography that creates misleading impressions about the product's size, quality, or function

If a customer receives an item that materially differs from the listing images, they are entitled to a return and full refund under the "Item Not as Described" provisions of the Returns and Refunds Policy, regardless of the standard return window.

## Section 2: Marketplace Seller Listing Obligations

Marketplace sellers must adhere to the same listing standards as first-party products, with additional requirements:

### 2.1 Seller-Specific Requirements
1. Sellers must accurately describe the item's condition (new, refurbished, used, open-box)
2. Sellers must disclose any known defects, wear, or imperfections for non-new items
3. Sellers must list their return policy clearly on each product listing
4. Sellers must specify their shipping origin and estimated delivery timeline
5. Sellers must not use stock photos for used or refurbished items — actual product photos are required

### 2.2 Listing Violations
If a marketplace seller's listing is found to be inaccurate or misleading:
1. **First offense**: Warning and listing correction required within 24 hours
2. **Second offense**: Listing removed and seller receives a performance strike
3. **Third offense within 90 days**: Seller account subject to temporary suspension (7–30 days)
4. **Pattern of violations**: Seller account may be permanently terminated

Customers who are affected by inaccurate listings are covered by the Marketplace Guarantee regardless of the seller's return policy.

## Section 3: Price Accuracy and Pricing Disputes

### 3.1 Price Display Requirements
1. All prices must be displayed in the local currency of the customer's region
2. Prices must include all mandatory fees (environmental fees, base charges)
3. Taxes and shipping are calculated and displayed at checkout before the customer confirms the purchase
4. Any price changes after the customer adds an item to the cart must be clearly communicated before checkout

### 3.2 Pricing Error Response
If a pricing error is discovered after orders have been placed:
1. The platform will evaluate whether to honor the incorrect price or cancel the affected orders
2. For minor pricing errors (within 10% of the correct price), the platform will typically honor the listed price
3. For significant pricing errors (more than 10% below the correct price), the platform may:
   - Cancel the orders with a full refund and explanation
   - Offer the customer the item at the correct price with a 10% courtesy discount
   - Honor the price for orders that have already shipped
4. In all cases, the customer will be notified of the pricing error and given options
5. The platform will never charge a customer more than the price shown at checkout

### 3.3 Price Matching
**The platform does not offer price matching with competitors.** If a customer requests a price match:
1. Inform the customer that price matching is not available
2. Do not attempt to match prices, issue credits, or make exceptions
3. If the customer has already purchased the item and finds it cheaper elsewhere, no adjustment will be made
4. If the customer finds the same item at a lower price on the platform itself within 7 days of purchase, a price adjustment may be requested — this is handled as a **price protection claim**, not a price match

**Price protection**: If the same item (same SKU, sold by the same seller) drops in price within 7 days of the customer's purchase, the customer may request a refund of the price difference. This applies only to first-party items and requires the lower price to still be available at the time of the claim.

## Section 4: Product Safety Compliance

All products listed on the platform must comply with applicable safety regulations:

### 4.1 First-Party Products
1. All first-party products undergo safety compliance review before listing
2. Products subject to safety recalls are immediately delisted and affected customers are notified
3. Products that require safety certifications (UL listing, CE marking, FCC compliance) must have valid certifications on file

### 4.2 Marketplace Products
1. Marketplace sellers certify compliance with applicable safety regulations as part of their seller agreement
2. The platform monitors for safety concerns through customer reports, regulatory databases, and proactive surveillance
3. Products reported as safety hazards are temporarily delisted pending investigation
4. Sellers listing products that violate safety regulations are subject to immediate account suspension

### 4.3 Customer Safety Reports
If a customer reports a safety concern about a product:
1. The report is escalated immediately to the product safety team
2. The product listing may be temporarily suspended pending investigation
3. The customer receives a full refund regardless of the standard return policy
4. If the safety concern is confirmed, all customers who purchased the product are notified
5. The platform cooperates with regulatory authorities if the safety issue triggers a formal investigation or recall

## Section 5: Intellectual Property and Counterfeit Products

### 5.1 Counterfeit Product Policy
The platform has zero tolerance for counterfeit products:
1. Any product confirmed as counterfeit will be immediately removed and the seller permanently banned
2. Customers who received counterfeit products are entitled to a full refund regardless of any return window
3. The platform will report confirmed counterfeiting to law enforcement and brand owners
4. Customer reports of suspected counterfeit products are investigated by the brand protection team within 48 hours

### 5.2 Customer Claims of Counterfeit
If a customer claims they received a counterfeit product:
1. Request evidence (photographs showing discrepancies with authentic products)
2. Process a full refund and return per standard policy
3. Submit the product for investigation by the brand protection team
4. If the counterfeit claim is confirmed, take action against the seller
5. If the claim cannot be verified, the standard return and refund process applies

## Section 6: Listing Updates and Transparency

### 6.1 Material Changes
If a product listing is materially updated after orders have been placed (e.g., specifications changed, features removed):
1. Existing orders are fulfilled based on the listing at the time of purchase
2. If the material change makes the product less desirable, affected customers must be notified and given the option to cancel for a full refund
3. Post-update customer claims of "item not as described" will be evaluated against the listing at the time of their purchase, not the current listing

### 6.2 Review and Rating Integrity
1. Product reviews and ratings must reflect genuine customer experiences
2. Sellers may not incentivize reviews (offering discounts, free products, or compensation for positive reviews)
3. Reviews that violate the platform's review policy (fake reviews, competitor sabotage, incentivized reviews) will be removed
4. Customers should not be penalized or have their accounts restricted for leaving honest negative reviews
