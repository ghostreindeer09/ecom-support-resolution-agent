# Order Cancellation Policy

**Document ID:** POL-CAN-003
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All first-party and marketplace orders

---

## Section 1: Pre-Shipment Cancellation

Customers may cancel any first-party order at no cost before the order has been shipped. The cancellation window depends on the order status:

1. **Order status "placed"**: Cancellation is guaranteed. The order has been received but not yet entered the fulfillment pipeline. Full refund will be issued within 3–5 business days to the original payment method.
2. **Order status "processing"**: Cancellation is available but may not be possible if the item has already been picked and packed. The system will attempt to cancel the order, and the customer will receive confirmation within 1–2 hours. If the item has already been packed, the customer will be notified and may need to refuse delivery or initiate a return after receiving the item.
3. **Order status "shipped"**: The order cannot be cancelled once it has been handed off to the carrier. The customer must wait to receive the item and then initiate a return per the Returns and Refunds Policy.

For marketplace seller orders, cancellation is subject to the seller's cancellation policy, which is displayed on the product listing page. If the marketplace seller does not have a cancellation policy listed, the platform's standard pre-shipment cancellation policy applies.

---

## Section 2: Post-Shipment Cancellation

Once an order has been shipped, it cannot be cancelled through the platform. The customer has two options:

1. **Refuse delivery**: If the customer refuses the package upon delivery, it will be returned to the fulfillment center. A refund will be processed once the item is received and inspected, minus any applicable return shipping costs (unless the item is eligible for free returns).
2. **Accept and return**: The customer may accept the delivery and then initiate a standard return per the Returns and Refunds Policy.

Important: If a customer refuses delivery of a perishable item, no refund will be issued for the perishable components of the order, as the items cannot be restocked or resold.

---

## Section 3: Cancellation of Promotional Orders

Orders placed with a promotional code or during a sale event follow the same cancellation rules with the following additions:

1. If a promotional order is cancelled and the customer wishes to re-order, the promotional code may no longer be valid if the promotional period has ended
2. The refund for a promotional order will reflect the actual amount charged, not the original retail price
3. If a promotional order included a free gift, the gift must also be returned to receive a full refund

---

## Section 4: Cancellation of Subscription Orders

Subscription cancellations and modifications follow the Subscription section of the Returns and Refunds Policy. Cancellation of individual subscription deliveries may be possible if the item has not entered the fulfillment pipeline for the current cycle.

---

## Section 5: Automatic Cancellation

The platform may automatically cancel an order in the following circumstances without customer request:

1. Payment authorization failure or fraud detection
2. Item is out of stock and cannot be sourced within the stated delivery window
3. Pricing error that resulted in an item being listed at a significantly incorrect price
4. Regulatory compliance issue (e.g., item cannot be shipped to the customer's location)

In all cases of automatic cancellation, the customer will be notified via email and will receive a full refund within 3–5 business days. No restocking fees or penalties will apply to automatically cancelled orders.
