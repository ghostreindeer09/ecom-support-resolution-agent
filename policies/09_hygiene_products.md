# Hygiene Products Policy

**Document ID:** POL-HYG-009
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All hygiene and personal care products

---

## Section 1: Definition of Hygiene Products

The following categories are classified as hygiene products and are subject to special return restrictions:

1. **Electric personal care devices**: Electric toothbrushes, electric razors and shavers, hair removal devices, facial cleansing devices
2. **In-ear audio devices**: Earbuds, in-ear headphones, hearing aids
3. **Intimates and undergarments**: Underwear, swimwear, intimate apparel
4. **Skincare and beauty devices**: Derma rollers, facial steamers, LED therapy masks (if seal is broken)
5. **Oral care products**: Manual toothbrushes, water flossers, mouthguards

---

## Section 2: Return Restrictions

Hygiene products that have been **opened, used, or had their seal broken** are classified as **non-returnable**. This restriction exists for health and safety reasons, as these items cannot be resold or restocked once opened.

Specifically:
1. Items with an intact, unopened seal may be returned within the standard 30-day return window
2. Items with a broken seal or visible signs of use are **final sale** — no refund or exchange will be provided
3. The non-returnable status must be clearly indicated on the product page and in the order confirmation
4. If a customer opens a hygiene product and claims it is defective, the claim will be reviewed but the item is still non-returnable per this policy. The customer may be directed to the manufacturer's warranty process.

---

## Section 3: Defective Hygiene Products

If a customer claims a hygiene product is defective:

1. If the item is **unopened**: Return and refund per standard policy
2. If the item is **opened**:
   - The customer must provide a description of the defect and photographic/video evidence
   - If the defect is confirmed as a manufacturing issue, the customer may receive **store credit** (not a refund to the original payment method) equal to the purchase price
   - The defective opened item does not need to be returned
   - If the defect cannot be confirmed, no refund or credit will be issued
   - Complex defect claims for opened hygiene items should be escalated to a supervisor

---

## Section 4: Hygiene Product Recalls

In the event of a manufacturer recall affecting hygiene products:

1. All customers who purchased the recalled item will be notified
2. Recalled items must be returned regardless of open/used status
3. Full refunds will be issued for recalled items regardless of the return window
4. Return shipping for recalled items is covered by the platform
