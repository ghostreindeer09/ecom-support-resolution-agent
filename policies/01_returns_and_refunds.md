# Returns and Refunds Policy

**Document ID:** POL-RR-001
**Effective Date:** January 1, 2026
**Last Updated:** March 15, 2026
**Applies To:** All first-party orders placed through the platform

---

## Section 1: General Return Window

All items sold and fulfilled by {Platform Name} ("first-party orders") are eligible for return within **30 calendar days** from the confirmed delivery date. The return window begins on the date the carrier confirms delivery, as reflected in the order tracking system. Returns requested after the 30-day window has closed will not be processed, and no exceptions will be made unless the item falls under a specific exception category outlined in this document.

To initiate a return, the customer must contact customer support or use the self-service return portal. A Return Merchandise Authorization (RMA) number will be issued, and the customer must ship the item back within 10 business days of receiving the RMA. Items returned without an RMA will be refused at the warehouse and returned to the customer at their expense.

The item must be returned in its original packaging, including all accessories, manuals, and documentation. Items that are missing components may be assessed a restocking fee of up to 15% of the purchase price. If the original packaging has been discarded, the customer must use a protective shipping container to prevent damage during transit. Items damaged during return shipping due to inadequate packaging are the responsibility of the customer and may result in a reduced refund or denial.

---

## Section 2: Refund Processing

Once a returned item is received at our fulfillment center and passes inspection, the refund will be processed within **5–10 business days**. Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. If the original payment method is no longer available (e.g., expired credit card), the refund will be issued as store credit.

Refund amounts are calculated based on the item purchase price, excluding original shipping charges unless the return is due to a platform error (wrong item shipped, defective item, or item not as described). In cases where the platform is at fault, the full purchase price plus original shipping costs will be refunded.

**Partial refunds** may be issued in the following circumstances:
- Item returned with visible signs of use beyond initial inspection
- Missing accessories or packaging materials
- Item returned outside the 30-day window but within 45 days (at the discretion of a supervisor, subject to a 20% restocking fee)
- Promotional or discounted items may receive a refund based on the actual amount paid, not the original retail price

---

## Section 3: Damaged Items

If a customer receives an item that is damaged during shipping, they are eligible for a **full refund or free replacement** at their choice. Damage claims must be filed within **7 calendar days** of the confirmed delivery date. After 7 days, damage claims will be reviewed on a case-by-case basis and may be denied if the damage cannot be reasonably attributed to shipping.

To file a damage claim, the customer must provide:
1. A photograph of the damaged item
2. A photograph of the shipping packaging showing any visible damage
3. The order number and delivery date

Upon verification, the customer may choose to receive a full refund or a replacement item. If a replacement is chosen and the original item is in stock, we will ship the replacement within 2–3 business days at no additional cost. If the item is out of stock, a full refund will be issued instead.

Customers are not required to return damaged items valued at under $50. For items valued at $50 or more, a prepaid return shipping label will be provided, and the damaged item must be returned within 10 business days.

---

## Section 4: Wrong Item Received

If a customer receives an item that does not match their order (wrong product, wrong size, wrong color, wrong model), they are eligible for a **free replacement or full refund** at their choice. Wrong item claims must be submitted within **14 calendar days** of the confirmed delivery date.

The customer must provide:
1. A photograph of the item received
2. A description of the item they expected to receive
3. The order number

A prepaid return shipping label will be provided for the incorrect item. The replacement will be shipped upon confirmation that the return label has been generated, without waiting for the incorrect item to arrive at the warehouse.

If the correct item is out of stock, a full refund will be issued plus a **10% goodwill credit** applied as store credit for the inconvenience.

---

## Section 5: Defective Items

Items that are found to be defective upon unboxing or within **30 days** of delivery are eligible for a full refund or replacement. A defect is defined as a manufacturing issue that prevents the item from functioning as described in the product listing.

Defect claims must be accompanied by:
1. A description of the defect
2. A photograph or video demonstrating the defect
3. Confirmation that the defect was not caused by customer misuse

For electronics, if the defect manifests after 30 days but within the manufacturer's warranty period, the customer should be directed to the manufacturer's warranty process. The platform does not process warranty claims on behalf of manufacturers after the 30-day return window.

**Important exception:** If the item category is "software" or "digital product" with a broken seal or activated license key, the item is classified as non-returnable regardless of defect claims. Customers with defective software should be directed to the software publisher's support channel.

---

## Section 6: Non-Returnable Items

The following categories of items are classified as **non-returnable** and are not eligible for refund under any circumstances (except as required by applicable law):

1. **Hygiene products** that have been opened, unsealed, or used — including but not limited to electric toothbrushes, razors, personal care devices, earbuds (in-ear), and intimates
2. **Gift cards** — gift cards are non-refundable and cannot be exchanged for cash or credit
3. **Digital downloads and software** with activated license keys or broken seals
4. **Customized or personalized items** made to the customer's specifications
5. **Hazardous materials** including batteries, chemicals, and flammable goods
6. **Items marked as "Final Sale"** — items purchased during final sale events or marked with a final sale designation at the time of purchase are non-returnable and non-refundable. Final sale status is displayed on the product page and confirmed in the order confirmation email.

If a customer receives a non-returnable item that is defective or damaged in transit, they may be eligible for a replacement or store credit. These cases should be escalated to a supervisor for review.

---

## Section 7: Restocking Fees

A **restocking fee** may be applied in the following scenarios:

1. **Electronics returned after opening** — up to 15% restocking fee may apply if the item has been opened but is otherwise functional and undamaged
2. **Large items or furniture** — a restocking fee of up to 20% may apply to cover return shipping and handling costs
3. **Returns beyond 30 days but within 45 days** — if approved by a supervisor, a 20% restocking fee will apply
4. **Missing original packaging or accessories** — a deduction equivalent to the retail value of missing components will be applied

The restocking fee will be deducted from the refund amount. The restocking fee policy must be disclosed to the customer before they initiate the return. If a restocking fee was not disclosed at the point of sale or in the order confirmation, the customer may dispute the fee. Disputed restocking fees should be escalated to a manager for resolution.

---

## Section 8: Subscription Services

For subscription-based products and services:

1. Customers may cancel their subscription at any time through the self-service portal or by contacting support
2. Cancellations made before the next billing cycle will prevent future charges
3. **Refunds for past subscription periods** are generally not provided unless the customer can demonstrate that the service was not delivered as promised
4. If a subscription box contains incorrect or missing items, the customer is eligible for a replacement shipment or a credit toward the next subscription period
5. If incorrect items are delivered in multiple consecutive subscription periods (2 or more), the issue should be escalated to account management for investigation and the customer is eligible for a refund of at least the most recent incorrect shipment
6. Subscription cancellation refunds, if approved, are prorated based on the number of days remaining in the current billing period

---

## Section 9: Multi-Order Disputes

When a customer disputes multiple orders or requests refunds across several transactions:

1. Individual refund requests should be evaluated on their own merit per the standard return policy
2. If a pattern of frequent returns emerges (more than 3 refund requests within a 90-day period), the account should be flagged for review
3. Multi-order refund requests totaling more than $500 require supervisor approval
4. Patterns suggesting abuse of the return policy (such as ordering an item, using it, and returning it repeatedly) should be escalated to the fraud prevention team
5. Customers with flagged accounts retain all their standard consumer rights but may be subject to additional verification requirements for future returns
